Welcome to NITSA

Commandant's Welcome Address to NITSA — Introduction

On behalf of the Comptroller General of Immigration Service, Mr Muhammed Babandede, MFR, the DG Human Resource and the ACG Training and Development, I the commandant wish to welcome you to the maiden edition of NITSA Website. The Website will feature a concise synopsis of past and present activities of the school;database for staff and students of the school etc.

The aim of this initiative is to key into the digitilisation process embarked upon by the service, especially in the training schools. Among the advantages of the initiatives is to ease the exchange of information between the school and the service headquarters it will also eliminate the manual process of registration when the new course commences. Enjoy!
